Every Main Bond Actors

All the Bond actors in this list have been portrayed in the popular James Bond movies produced by Eon Productions. Eon produced films that hit a combined gross of nearly $7 billion, making it the sixth highest grossing film series.

Sean Connery (1962-1981)

Sean Connery is the actor that defined and made James Bond what it is now today. All seven of Connery's James Bond films were a smashing hit, which led James Bond to continue to what it is nearly 58 years later. Ironically enough, Ian Fleming did not view Connery as suitable for James Bond due to his appearance looking like an "overgrown stunt-man."

George Lazenby (1969)

George Lazenby is the 2nd actor to become the iconic James Bond and was the youngest to become James bond. Only portraying in one film, Lazenby is the least popular and the most forgettable James Bond actor to this date. After his first film, he decides to quit and move on with other things. This ultimately led to this career downfall.

Roger Moore (1973-1985)

After Connery's depature from James Bond and Lazenby's disappearance, Roger Moore steps in to play James Bond for the next 12 years. Moore's approach to James Bond is more charismatic, humorous, and less cold blooded than Connery's. Moore still hits the record as the oldest James Bond to this date, which did cause some controversy among the audience.

Timothy Dalton (1987-1989)

After Moore's era came to end, James Bond needed a new actor that would shake up the style and be more serious. Timothy Dalton, a long time Bond fan and reader of the novels, comes into the scene as a more cold blooded and gritty James Bond. Out of all the other Bond actors, Dalton still holds up as the most faithful to the Bond novels. Unfortunately, Dalton's career becomes short-sighted as his two films are at the bottom of the highest grossing bond films.

Pierce Brosnan (1995-2002)

Originally, Pierce Brosnan was meant to take the role as James Bond before Timothy Dalton became Bond. But, due to contract issues, he was not able to become Bond until 1995. His portrayal as Bond became a smashing hit, leading to the creation of three more Bond films and one of the most iconic video games of all time, Goldeneye N64.

Daniel Craig (2006-2020)

The current James Bond, Daniel Craig, is approaching the end of his era. Craig portrays his Bond similar to Dalton's, dark and gritty. Based on his appearance, Craig received a lot of controversy back in his first film due to his blonde hair. However, times changed and his films are among the top best in the Bond list.
