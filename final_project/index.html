The Cold Blooded Spy

James Bond, codename 007, is a fictional character created by author Ian Fleming. Starring in Ian Fleming's 12 books and 25 movies, James Bond continues to be represented in the film series for over 58 years. Bond also features in non-canonical movies and books published after Ian Fleming's death.

007 is a British Secret Service agent working for MI6. In all novels and movies, Bond is the protagonist who is sent on missions to spy on countries and global terrorists. Bond's defining traits are drinking and love-making, driving cars, and smoking.

Ian Fleming, Creator
